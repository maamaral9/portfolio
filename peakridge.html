Ideation

When asked to come up with a new company it is very difficult because there is a lot of research that goes behind it. However, I have been going camping ever since I was a child. It was kind of like a family tradition. I still carry on this tradition and I also love to try different outdoor activities and keeping fit. I thought what better way then to have a website/app that allows Ontarians to find different campsites and outdoor activities at the click of a button.

Peak Ridge's primary goal is to allow the user to book a campsite either for pitching a tent or if they want to plan their next RV vacation. Indulge in fun activities or just enjoy the peaceful serenity. If the customer chooses not to stay at the campground; there are many different experiences that the business offers (kayaking, canoeing, mountain climbing, hiking etc).

Audience and Target

The primary audience and target would be individuals who are looking to explore the great outdoors and/or wanting to try a new outdoor activity, males and females ranging from ages 27-45. The secondary target market would be people who are interested in doing something outdoorsy, but not sure where to start or look. The website/app provides a great way to keep people involved in what Ontario great land has to offer.

The Design

The colours chosen are very tranquil and serene. Each colour can easily represent the four seasons and since Peak Ridge is open all year around it is very fitting. The typeface used is Garamond Premier Pro is a very mature and trustworthy font.

Hi-fidelity wireframes makes it easier to map out the layout of the design: which is meant to be clear and concise. When the user first lands on the application or website they will be greeted by information on the business and ways on how to reserve anywhere or anytime!


Client: Humber College





HOW IT WORKS

My final vision for Peak Ridge is a straight-forward website, where users can explore all the different campsites and/or outdoor experience Ontario has to offer. Once the user has found the campsite or outdoor activity of their choosing they can book or reserve online.

The app and website were user tested and each video shows how simple and easy it is to book with Peak Ridge.
