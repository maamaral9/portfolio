The Opportunity

It is difficult enough to go shopping at a mall and having to go into each store individually. You may need to buy yourself some shoes, a new dress, possibly an outfit for your significant other, oh yeah and don't forget to pick up some essentials for your home. Look no further because Delilah Fashion has everything you need and more!

When I was younger I would go to the mall with my friends and we could go shopping (or window shopping) for hours and hours. Unfortunately, as I grew older I noticed that my energy and patience for shopping diminished. When speaking with friends and family they too could not shop like they used to and I believe that's why online shopping is booming. Shopping from the comfort of your own home and in your pajamas? Count me in. That's why I wanted to design this company because not only does Delilah Fashion offer online shopping it also has everything you need for the family. Some categories consist of: Men's clothing, women's clothing, baby and children clothing, accessories, home essentials and much more!

Ideation

Looking at fashion companies that have survived throughout the years, it is clear what works and what does not. These stores mainly sell male, female, children and home essentials. When designing this store from scratch I really wanted to combine all of these elements into one clean package. Our main competitors would be Hudson's Bay and Nordstrom because they are department stores that offer designer names at lower than average prices.

Audience and Target

Of course it would be great to target everyone; however, it was quite clear that the target audience would be male/females between the ages of 25-40 years of age. Individuals who are not afraid to make a bold wardrobe statement and willing to spend a pretty penny on items they will love and cherish. Whether they are shopping for themselves, their homes, a loved one or just wanting to stay in style with their favourite brands: Delilah is the place to shop.

The Design

Delilah Fashion is a very vibrant, fun and friendly company. These colours definitely represent that because they are very inviting and bright, yet sophisticated. The primary colour of peach is very trendy and can be admired by any gender. The typeface Riesling used for the logo has large counters which allows the logo to breathe and appear more pleasing to the eye. The Oswald typeface is used for the body content which makes it easy to read on smaller devices.


User Flow

My main priority is making the user flow very simple and easy to use. When the user visits the landing page they are immediately shown the most featured categories. If a user knows exactly what he/she is looking for they have the search button located on the bottom right corner of the screen. The easier the app is for the user the faster they can locate the item they are looking for and check out. If the process is quick and painless to use then you can expect to see a returning customer. Another way of securing a returning customer is having the user create a login. At Delilah Fashion, if the customer creates a login they will obtain their digital VIP card, keep track of their rewards, manage order history and create a profile so that "saved items" can be retrieved easily.
