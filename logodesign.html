LOGO DESIGN

This is an independent school project that was over the span of 2 weeks. The objective of this project is to visualize the brand by creating a Logo Design for my company.
Peak Ridge is a company that is all about the outdoors: the primary goal is to allow the user to book a campsite either for pitching a tent or if they want to plan their next RV vacation.

Created using: Illustrator & Photoshop
