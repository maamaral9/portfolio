App Design

Come and celebrate mother nature with friends/family and enjoy the peaceful sounds of the river stream. Peak Ridge's primary goal is to allow the user to book a campsite either for pitching a tent or if they want to plan their next RV vacation. Indulge in fun activities or just enjoy the peaceful serenity. If the customer chooses not to stay at the campground; there are many different experiences that the business offers (kayaking, canoeing, mountain climbing, hiking etc). Hi-fidelity wireframes makes it easier to map out the layout of the design: which is meant to be clear and concise. When the user first lands on the application or website they will be greeted, followed by information on the business and ways on how to reserve anywhere or anytime!
